Mohr Design

Overview

Mohr Design is a boutique product design studio in NYC that specializes in Product strategy and product design for Fortune 500 companies. Some of their clients include Google, Reuters and TripAdvisor.

I supported their team over the past year and a half on various projects, focussing on UX concept explorations from sketches to final design. Core to the work was translating User Research insights into viable and desirable solutions to inform and set the strategy and vision for future experiences. I worked highly collaboratively with the Mohr Design team and the clients they supported, helping bring an outside perspective and opinion to the table.

Unfortunately due to the highly strategic nature of the work, it is confidential so cannot be shared. References are available upon request.
