Curbee app

Overview

Curbee needed a feature for customers to view their appointments and provide live tracking.

Using common tracking patterns from popular delivery and car service apps our users knew well, allowed for quick design, development and launch in just a few months.

The app is now the primary way users book appointments with Curbee.
