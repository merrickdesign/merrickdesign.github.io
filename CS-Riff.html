Riff

Overview

Riff Technologies, a venture-backed start-up, created Riff, a voice and video tool enabling remote teams to work like they are in the same room.

As the sole product designer, I designed the product from early prototypes to multiple releases. I conducted weekly user interviews and worked with the founders to prioritise what to build next.

After a year, the average user spent over 6hrs/day on Riff, comparable to Slack.
